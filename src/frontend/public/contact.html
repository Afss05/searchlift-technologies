FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from businesses exploring digital marketing services.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy — most clients begin seeing measurable improvements in rankings and organic traffic within 3–6 months. Significant growth typically accelerates from month 6 onwards as domain authority builds. We set honest expectations in every client proposal.

What makes SearchLift different from other agencies?

We're obsessively focused on business outcomes, not vanity metrics. Every campaign has clear KPIs tied to revenue — not just rankings. We also provide full transparency with monthly reports showing exactly what we've done and what it's achieved.

Do you work with businesses outside Chennai?

Yes — while our roots are in Chennai, we serve clients across India and internationally. We have active campaigns running for businesses in Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and several international markets. All client work is managed remotely with video calls and our project management platform.

What is included in the free SEO audit?

Our free audit covers technical SEO health (Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, site structure), keyword ranking analysis, competitor gap analysis, backlink profile review, and on-page optimisation opportunities. You'll receive a written report with prioritised recommendations — no sales pitch required.

What minimum budget do I need for Google Ads?

We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000/month to run effective Google Search campaigns for most industries in Chennai. Below this threshold, campaign learning is slow and results are inconsistent. Our management fee is separate and scales with your budget.
