Our Story

From a Small Studio to Chennai's Top Digital Agency

SearchLift Technologies was founded in 2014 with a single mission: help local businesses compete and win online. What started as a two-person SEO consultancy in Anna Nagar has grown into a full-service digital marketing powerhouse with 60+ specialists.

Over the past decade, we've worked with more than 500 businesses — from ambitious startups to established enterprises — achieving page-one rankings, reducing customer acquisition costs, and building lasting digital brands.

Today, SearchLift is recognised as one of Tamil Nadu's most awarded digital agencies, trusted by clients in healthcare, education, e-commerce, and beyond.
